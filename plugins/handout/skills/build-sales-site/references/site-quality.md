# Sales-site quality rubric

Score each dimension 0–2 before handoff. Fix any zero.

1. **Audience clarity** — A buyer can tell who the site is for and why it matters immediately.
2. **Narrative** — The content moves from context to outcome, approach, proof, plan, and next step without repetition.
3. **Specificity** — Claims use approved facts and concrete details; unknowns are omitted or framed honestly.
4. **Scanability** — Headings, paragraphs, cards, lists, and page boundaries make the site easy to skim.
5. **Actionability** — The next step has a clear owner, action, and working destination.
6. **Personalization** — Variables add relevance without changing the underlying truth or exposing data.
7. **Resilience** — Default values read naturally, links are valid, and the canonical page works without a variant.
8. **Trust** — No fabricated social proof, buyer intent, customer facts, commitments, or sensitive information.
9. **Technical validity** — The current schema and Tiptap structure pass Handout validation.
10. **State clarity** — The user knows whether the result is draft, shared, or published and receives the correct link.
